Why The Arc of DFW

A Track Record
No One Can Fake

The Arc of DFW Area has advocated for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities across 9 DFW counties since 1971. Every dollar raised through Putts for Progress goes directly to advocacy, training, and community programs for the IDD community and their families.

In just two years, Putts for Progress has raised $51,500 and reached 600+ people through programs. Sponsoring The Arc is one of the most credible community investments a DFW company can make.

Common Questions

What Companies
Ask Us

"We already support another nonprofit. Why The Arc?"
The Arc of DFW fills a gap most nonprofits don't touch — the systems-level advocacy that determines whether families with IDD can access housing, healthcare, education, and employment at all. In two years, Putts for Progress has raised $51,500 and reached 600+ people directly. This is not a duplicative investment. It's the infrastructure layer that makes everything else possible.
"How do we know this is a good use of our budget?"
Every dollar raised goes directly to programs and advocacy. The A Look Ahead Conference Series is now in its 11th year — 400+ families attended last year alone. The ACE Workshop and Digital Literacy Training are reaching 100+ individuals each. You can see exactly where the money goes. We can provide a post-event impact summary in a format ready for your internal communications and social channels.
"Will our team actually want to come to this?"
Putts for Progress is held at Puttshack Addison — tech-infused mini golf, great food, a full bar, and a competitive evening with a real cause behind it. Companies who have attended consistently find their teams asking to come back. This is not a formal gala. It is a genuinely fun evening that happens to change lives in DFW.
"We're interested but not ready to commit at the Club level."
The Hole Sponsorship at $500 is the right starting point. It puts your brand on the course, gets your name on the website, earns you stage recognition, and gives you a ticket to the event. Companies that start at the Hole level and experience the evening firsthand almost always return at a higher tier the following year.
