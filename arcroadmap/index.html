3–4 Story Video Volunteers — Confirmed and Ready to Film

All volunteers must have footage delivered to Joey no later than July 18. Filming can be done on a phone.
Ask: James or Volunteer Coordinator · Hard deadline: footage to JRZ by July 18
3
Assign the Internal Corporate Outreach Lead
Joey has built the full Corporate Outreach Cadence Playbook — 3-stage email sequence, LinkedIn stakeholder search terms, objection handling, and timing guide. The org needs to name one person who will execute this cadence. That person receives the playbook June 26 and begins prospecting immediately. Joey does not send outreach — the internal lead does, using JRZ's tools.
Ask: James · Name the internal outreach lead · Needed by June 26
4
Access to Arc's Social Accounts and NeonOne
Joey writes and schedules all social content and email campaigns. He needs admin access to The Arc's Facebook and LinkedIn accounts, or a reliable person inside the org who can publish content within 24 hours of receiving it. All email campaigns will be loaded into NeonOne — access or a designated sender is required before July 3.
Ask: James or Comms designee · Admin access or dedicated publisher · Needed before July 3
