What Joey Needs From The Org

The 4 Asks

Joey can build everything — but he can't manufacture these four things. These are the only items that require action from James, the board, or someone inside the organization. Everything else is handled.

4 Things the Org Needs to Provide

These are not ongoing responsibilities. They are one-time asks that unlock the campaign. The sooner these are confirmed, the sooner the full 8-week runway is in play.

1
A Single Point of Contact for Approvals
Joey will draft all content — social posts, emails, video scripts, outreach copy. Before anything goes out under The Arc's name, one person needs to review and approve it. This person needs to be reachable and able to turn approvals around within 48 hours. Without this, the content calendar stalls.
Ask: James or board designee · One-time setup
2
3–4 Story Video Volunteers
Joey will write the scripts, direct the shoots, and handle the edit brief. The org needs to identify 3–4 willing participants — a parent, a self-advocate, a board member or staff person, and optionally a program participant. Names and contact info is all Joey needs. Filming can be done on a phone.
Ask: James or Volunteer Coordinator · One-time ask
3
Corporate Sponsor Relationship Access
Joey can write every sponsor email and build every proposal — but a $2,000 Club Sponsor commitment closes faster when it comes from James or a board member personally, not a consultant. Joey needs to know: who on the board has existing relationships with DFW businesses? Which companies have given before? Those names and warm connections are the difference between a cold email and a closed sponsorship.
Ask: James / Board · Provide existing relationships and prior sponsor list
4
Access to Arc's Social Accounts and Email Platform
Joey will write and schedule all social content and email campaigns. To do that, he needs either posting access to The Arc's Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram accounts, or a reliable person inside the org who can publish content within 24 hours of receiving it. Same for the email platform — whether that's Mailchimp, Constant Contact, or whatever the org uses.
Ask: James or Comms designee · Admin access or dedicated publisher
