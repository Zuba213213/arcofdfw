Why The Arc of DFW

55 Years of Credibility
No One Can Fake

The Arc of DFW Area has advocated for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities across the DFW Metroplex since 1971. We are 100% volunteer-run. No paid staff. Every dollar raised goes directly to advocacy, training, and community programs for the IDD community and their families.

Sponsoring The Arc is one of the most credible community investments a DFW company can make — because the organization's 55-year track record speaks before you say a word.

Common Questions

What Companies
Ask Us

"We already support [other nonprofit]. Why The Arc?"
The Arc of DFW fills a specific gap that most nonprofits don't touch: the systems-level advocacy that determines whether families with IDD can access housing, healthcare, education, and employment at all. Other organizations provide direct services — The Arc changes the rules those services operate under. Sponsoring The Arc is not an either/or decision; it's the civic infrastructure layer that makes everything else possible.
"How do we know this is a good use of our CSR budget?"
The Arc of DFW is 100% volunteer-run — meaning your sponsorship dollars go to programs and advocacy, not administrative overhead. The organization has a 55-year track record of fiscal responsibility. Last year's single event raised $34,000. We can provide a post-event impact report showing exactly how your sponsorship was used and what it produced — in a format you can share internally and externally.
"Will our employees actually want to attend this?"
Putts for Progress is held at Puttshack Addison — one of the most popular entertainment venues in DFW. It's tech-infused mini golf, great food, a full bar, and a live tournament with prizes. Most companies who send a team find their employees asking to come back the following year. This is not a formal gala; it is a genuinely fun evening with a cause behind it — which is exactly the combination that drives employee engagement.
"$10,000 is a significant commitment. Can we start smaller?"
Absolutely. The Community Sponsor level at $2,500 is a meaningful first partnership — and companies that start at the Community level and see what the event delivers consistently move up in subsequent years. We'd rather have you in the room at any tier than miss the opportunity to show you what The Arc of DFW is about.
