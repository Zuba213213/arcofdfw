Before Anything Else

The Kickoff Meeting

Nothing in this roadmap moves until one meeting happens. 60 minutes. The right people in the room. Every phase gets a date and a name on it. This is the meeting that converts the vault from a document into a campaign.

Kickoff Meeting Agenda — 60 Minutes

Schedule within the next 7 days. Can be Zoom or in-person. Required attendees: Executive Director or Board Chair, Event Lead, Marketing or Communications Lead, and JRZ (if involved in execution).

  • ◆10 min —Confirm event date. Everything else dates backward from this. Nothing else matters until this is locked.
  • ◆10 min —Adopt the $42K north star goal officially. Align on the realistic scenario ($38–42K) as the working target.
  • ◆15 min —Assign a name to every phase and every major task in this roadmap. Ownerless tasks do not get done.
  • ◆10 min —Identify the first 5–10 corporate sponsor prospects by name. Assign who makes each call personally.
  • ◆10 min —Identify 3–4 family/advocate story video volunteers by name. Set a date to film each one.
  • ◆5 min —Set next check-in date — weekly or biweekly, same time, same people.
